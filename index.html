About Us

Staines Farm Shop is a high-end greengrocer situated in the heart of Whitstable’s bustling high street. We offer a huge variety of produce, incorporating local farms as much as possible. On top of that, we work with the very best suppliers to import quality produce from all over the world. We pride ourselves in being the absolute best at what we do, and it is our goal to make sure every customer’s needs are met when visiting our lovely shop
